Prof. Dr. Dilip Kumar Dhar

Professor, Department of Medicine

MBBS, FCPS(Medicine), MD(Internal Medicine)

Prof. Dr. Dilip Kumar Dhar is a senior and widely respected physician with more than 30 years of experience in the field of Internal Medicine. Known for his diagnostic accuracy, calm demeanor, and academic leadership, he has contributed extensively to both patient care and medical education in Bangladesh. His practice spans across a wide range of medical conditions, from chronic diseases to acute medical emergencies. In his long and respected career, he has served as Principal and Head of Medicine at several leading private medical colleges in Bangladesh, including Delta Medical College, Anwer Khan Modern Medical College, and MH Samorita Medical College and hospital.

He earned his MBBS from Dhaka Medical College, followed by FCPS in Medicine from the Bangladesh College of Physicians and Surgeons (BCPS) and MD in Internal Medicine from Bangladesh Medical University (BMU). He currently serves as Professor of the Department of Medicine at MH Samorita Hospital & Medical College.
