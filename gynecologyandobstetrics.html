Excellence in Women's Health
Faculty of Gynecology & Obstetrics

The Faculty of Gynecology & Obstetrics at MH Samorita Medical College is led by seasoned academics and clinicians such as Prof. Dr. Nahla Bari (Professor & Head) and Prof. Dr. Mst. Bilkis Pervin, supported by skilled assistant professors and registrars. They oversee a comprehensive clinical and academic training program for MBBS students. The department emphasizes hands-on exposure to labor wards, antenatal clinics, and operating theaters, ensuring students gain real-world proficiency in managing normal and high-risk pregnancies, gynecological surgeries, infertility cases, and laparoscopic procedures.

In addition to routine clinical teaching, the faculty organizes regular seminars, case presentations, and workshops focused on reproductive health, gynecologic oncology, and advanced surgical techniques. These initiatives prepare students not only for Bangladesh’s diverse healthcare needs but also for postgraduate pathways such as FCPS and international exams (e.g., Indian PG exams) . With a well-equipped teaching hospital offering ICU, NICU, HDU, and 500-bed capacity, students pursue a 5-year MBBS degree backed by strong clinical infrastructure and expert guidance.
